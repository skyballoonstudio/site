Rhema

Personalized Bible verses for everyday life — in your pocket

 

Rhema whatnow?

Rhema is a Greek word that means "living voice of God". This app brings Rhema Cards, collections of categorized and paraphrased Bible verses (created by Dr. Wendell Smith), to the iPhone and iPod touch.

For years, Rhema Cards have been used worldwide for reference, encouragement and spiritual growth. Now the Rhema app makes them accessible to you in every situation, no matter where you are!

A library in your pocket

Rhema is a quick, light weight app designed with speed and accessibility in mind. It contains paraphrased verses organized by relevant topics:

  • Identity in Christ
  • Healing of Infirmities
  • Faith
  • No Fear
  • The Lord is My Strength
  • Wisdom and the Will of God
  • Passion for Purity
  • Joy is a Choice
  • Overcoming
  • Prosperity with a Purpose
  • Walking in Love
  • God Wants All Men to be Saved

You can browse by topic, mark your favorite verses, and search the entire catalog for a word or phrase. If you want to share a verse with a friend, you can email it, or copy it in order to draft a text message or tweet.
